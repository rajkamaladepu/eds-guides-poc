Behavior of Mail App Bar (Exchange Office Mail App setting)

Description
Determines what happens when a user clicks the Office Mail App bar in a shortcut: show the available Enterprise Vault options and immediately open the item in a new window, or show the Enterprise Vault options without opening the item.
Supported values
  • Open immediately and show options.
  • Show options (default).
