Archive draft items (Exchange Archiving General setting)

Description
Controls whether draft items are archived. This applies to all draft items, not just those in the Drafts folder.
Enterprise Vault does not convert archived draft items to shortcuts, even if the policy is configured to create shortcuts. Archived draft items are treated as shortcuts for purposes of shortcut deletion. Enterprise Vault does not remove attachments from archived draft items.
Supported values
  • Off (default). Draft items not archived.
  • On. Draft items are archived.
