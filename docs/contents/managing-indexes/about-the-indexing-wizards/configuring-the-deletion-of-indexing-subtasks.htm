Configuring the deletion of indexing subtasks

Enterprise Vault automatically deletes indexing subtasks that have completed with no errors, or have completed with warnings. By default, Enterprise Vault deletes these subtasks seven days after their completion. Enterprise Vault does not automatically delete subtasks that have failed.
You can change the number of days that elapse before deletion of completed subtasks in Enterprise Vault's site properties.
To change the number of days after which Enterprise Vault deletes indexing subtasks
  1. In the Administration Console, open Site Properties.
  2. Click the Indexing tab.
  3. Change the Delete indexing subtasks after value.
