Do not archive pending reminders (Exchange Archiving General setting)

Description
Controls whether Enterprise Vault archives items that have pending reminders.
Supported values
  • Off. Items that have pending reminders are archived.
  • On (default). Items that have pending reminders within the next five years are not archived.
Legacy name
DontArchiveItemsPendingReminder
