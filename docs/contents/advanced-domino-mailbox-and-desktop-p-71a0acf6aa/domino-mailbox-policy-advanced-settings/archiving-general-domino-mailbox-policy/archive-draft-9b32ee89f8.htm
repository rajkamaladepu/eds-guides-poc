Archive draft items (Domino Archiving General setting)

Description
Lets you control whether draft items are archived. Changes to the setting are applied to users' mail files when the Domino Provisioning task next runs. To apply the changes immediately, click Synchronize Individual Mailboxes on the Synchronization tab of the Domino Provisioning task properties.
Supported values
  • Off (default). Draft items are not archived.
  • On. Draft items are archived. When draft items are archived, they never change into shortcuts, and attachments are not removed.
