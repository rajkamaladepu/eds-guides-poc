Action for failed items (Domino Archiving General setting)

Description
Lets you control whether items that fail three times to be archived are processed on later archiving runs.
Supported values
  • Reprocess. Items that have failed repeatedly are reprocessed on each archiving run.
  • Do not reprocess (default). Items that have failed repeatedly are not reprocessed on later archiving runs.
Legacy name
SetFailedMsgsDoNotArchive
