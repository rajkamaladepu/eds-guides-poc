Lookup e-mail addresses (Domino Archiving General setting)

Description
Controls whether email addresses are resolved using the Domino Directory, including SMTP addresses where available.
Supported values
  • On (default). Email addresses are resolved.
  • Off. Email addresses are not resolved.
