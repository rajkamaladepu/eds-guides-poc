About expiry and deletion

Enterprise Vault can automatically perform the following:
Storage expiry
Delete items from archives when the items' retention periods have expired.
Shortcut deletion
Delete shortcuts from users' Exchange mailbox folders and Exchange public folders. You can configure Enterprise Vault to delete shortcuts over a certain age, or shortcuts to items that have been deleted from an archive.
