About moving journal archives within a site

Move Archive lets you move Domino and Exchange journal archives only within an Enterprise Vault site, and you cannot move a journal archive that is currently in use by the journaling task. When you want to move a source journal archive that is in use, first you must configure the journaling task to use a new archive in the destination vault store. To change the archive that is used by the journaling task, use the Administration Console to edit the journal mailbox properties.
You can move an inactive journal archive to a new archive, to an existing active archive, or to another inactive archive.
