Move Archive and user-deleted items

Enterprise Vault lets you retain items that users delete from their archives so that they are available for recovery for a period of time. You can enable this feature using the Enable recovery of user deleted items option on the Site Properties: Archive Settings tab. Enterprise Vault then retains user-deleted items for the number of days that you set.
Note:
During a move operation, Move Archive does not copy these deleted items from the source archive to the destination. As a result, the deleted items are not available for recovery from the destination archive.
